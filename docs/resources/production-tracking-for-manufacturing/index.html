Make status meaningful, not cosmetic

A useful status update tells the buyer what changed, what is blocked, what is next, and whether the delivery path is still intact. Taktum ties status to job milestones so updates are more than generic progress labels.

FAQ

What is manufacturing production tracking?

Manufacturing production tracking is the process of following job status through scheduling, production, quality, shipment, payment, and fulfillment milestones.

Does Taktum include production tracking?

Yes. Taktum connects production tracking to RFQs, quotes, payment records, fulfillment, and manufacturer performance.

What should a manufacturing production update include?

A production update should include the current stage, completed milestone, next step, known blockers, quality or inspection notes, delivery impact, and any action needed from the buyer or manufacturer.

Why does production tracking matter for supplier performance?

Production tracking records delivery, communication, issue resolution, and quality outcomes that can later support supplier qualification and capability-specific performance scoring.

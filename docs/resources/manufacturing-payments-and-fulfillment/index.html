FAQ

Why connect manufacturing payments to production tracking?

Connecting payments to production tracking helps customers and manufacturers understand what has been approved, paid, produced, shipped, and fulfilled.

Does Taktum support payments and fulfillment records?

Taktum is designed to keep quoting, payment records, production updates, shipping or fulfillment status, and supplier performance in the same operating layer.

What payment records matter in custom manufacturing?

Important payment records include quote acceptance, deposits, invoices, approvals, payment intent or settlement status, final balance, refunds or adjustments, and the fulfillment milestone tied to each payment.

How does fulfillment visibility help buyers?

Fulfillment visibility helps buyers see whether work has shipped, what delivery path is expected, whether there are blockers, and how the completed job should affect future supplier confidence.

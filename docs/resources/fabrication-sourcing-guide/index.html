FAQ

What should be included in a fabrication RFQ?

A fabrication RFQ should include drawings, materials, dimensions, tolerances, weld requirements, finish expectations, quantities, due dates, and inspection needs.

Can Taktum help source fabrication work?

Yes. Taktum routes fabrication requests to qualified manufacturers and keeps quoting, production tracking, payment records, and fulfillment visible.

How can buyers avoid poor fabrication quotes?

Buyers can avoid poor fabrication quotes by including complete drawings, material and finish requirements, weld details, quantities, inspection needs, delivery constraints, and any known manufacturability risks.

Why is fabrication sourcing different from buying catalog parts?

Fabrication sourcing depends on interpreting custom requirements, process capability, shop capacity, quality controls, and delivery constraints for a specific build rather than selecting a standard inventory item.
